ABOUT

Dpath Adviser is a mobile app which provides animated location of the stall of an organization or office in an organization where public activity is more or any organization which want to provide their customers or users location guide.

FEATURES

  • Search the organization/mall.
  • Organization/mall can have different password for each stall/office and change password as per their desire.
  • Every organization and mall can control their content and information. Even an app developer will not be able to see that information until they enter the password.
  • Provide the direction to the destination.
